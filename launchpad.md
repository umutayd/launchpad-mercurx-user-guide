# 🚀 Launchpad

The following are available on [this screen](https://launchpad.mercurx.com/launchpad) ;

1. There is a register and kyc, verify wallet and sell section.
2. There are active sales and completed sales.

SIGN UP AND KYC To get involved in MercurX sales, the first step is to complete the signup and KYC process.

VERIFY WALLET After successfully registering and providing your KYC information, wallet verification is the next step.

STAKE MERX Allocations in IDOs can be earned by staking MERX. Even if sales participation isn't your preference, staking could still yield benefits for you.

REGISTER FOR SALE During registration, confirm your intent to join. After it ends, no new registrations are accepted until the next sale.
